How it works

From agent noise to clear executive briefings

1

Collect

Voicer gathers blockers, decision requests, and status updates across your agents into a single agenda.

2

Brief

Each issue opens as a navigable tree: summary, root cause, attempts, options, recommendation, and decision node.

3

Decide

Say “choose option 2”, “go deeper”, or “skip”. Voicer captures intent, persists context, and moves to the next item.

4

Execute

Decisions route back to your agents through OpenClaw sessions so work keeps flowing without manual handoffs.
