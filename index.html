How it works

From channel noise to clear action

1

Connect

Link your Slack workspace, Gmail, or any MCP-compatible tool. One click, no tokens to paste.

2

Brief me

Put in your AirPods and say "brief me." AIPA tells you who said what, what needs a reply, and what's urgent.

3

Draft & approve

AIPA drafts replies in your voice. Approve by voice, edit, or reject. Over time, drafts match your style automatically.

4

Let it run

As trust builds, enable auto-reply on channels you choose. AIPA becomes your digital twin for routine communication.
