How it works

From channel noise to clear action

1

Connect

Add your Slack and Discord bot tokens. Create agents and link them to the channels they should watch.

2

Talk

Start a voice session. AIPA reads your channels, identifies what needs attention, and briefs you conversationally.

3

Decide

AIPA proposes messages to send. Approve them as-is, edit the wording, or reject. You stay in control.

4

Send

Approved messages go straight to Slack or Discord. Reply to threads, post updates, and keep work flowing hands-free.
