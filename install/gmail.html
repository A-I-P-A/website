Get voice briefings on your inbox and draft email replies hands-free.

Gmail Connection

AIPA connects to your Gmail account using Google OAuth. It can read your emails and send replies on your behalf.

Scopes: gmail.readonly, gmail.send, gmail.modify

Connect Gmail

What happens after connecting

  • AIPA securely stores a refresh token to access your Gmail
  • Open the AIPA iOS app, your Gmail appears as a connection
  • Create an agent and link it to Gmail labels (INBOX, etc.)
  • Ask your agent: "What's in my inbox?" or "Any emails from Sarah?"
  • Draft replies by voice: "Reply to Sarah saying I'll be there at 3"

What AIPA can do with Gmail

  • Read emails from any label (INBOX, Sent, custom labels)
  • Summarize email threads and highlight what needs attention
  • Draft and send replies (with your approval)
  • Cross-reference email with your Slack activity for context

Privacy

Emails are fetched directly from Gmail's API to your device. AIPA does not store your email content on any server. See our Privacy Policy.
