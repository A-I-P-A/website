Gmail Connection

AIPA connects to your Gmail via Google OAuth. It can read your emails, summarize threads, and send replies on your behalf.

Connect Gmail

After connecting

  • Open the AIPA app on your iPhone
  • Your Gmail appears as a connection
  • Create an agent and link it to Gmail labels (INBOX, etc.)
  • Ask: "What's in my inbox?" or "Any emails from Sarah?"
  • Draft replies by voice: "Reply to Sarah saying I'll be there at 3"

What AIPA does with Gmail

  • Reads emails from any label
  • Summarizes threads and highlights what needs attention
  • Drafts and sends replies (with your approval)
  • Cross-references email with your Slack for context

Privacy

Emails go directly from Gmail to your device. AIPA does not store your email on any server. Privacy Policy
